Event names:


Annual Charity Gala


The Annual Charity Gala is a formal event that gathers donors, volunteers, and supporters to raise funds for the orphanage.
It includes a dinner, live performances, speeches from influential figures, and a silent auction featuring donated items.
The event is designed to spread awareness about the orphanage’s mission and provide an opportunity for the community to contribute.

Toy Drive


The Toy Drive is an event where the community is encouraged to donate new toys to bring joy to the children at the orphanage.
During the holiday season or back-to-school time, this event helps provide children with presents they would not otherwise receive,
brightening their day and fostering a sense of community.

Volunteer Day


Volunteer Day invites individuals or groups to spend a day volunteering at the orphanage.
Activities may include playing with children, helping with maintenance or gardening, organizing educational workshops, or preparing meals.
This day is a way for people to engage directly with the children and make a meaningful impact.

Back-to-School Fundraiser


Cooking for a Cause is an event where local chefs or community members gather to prepare meals for the children at the orphanage.
The event could also be expanded to offer cooking classes for the public,
with a portion of the proceeds going to support the orphanage’s programs. It’s a fun way to bring people together for a good cause while teaching valuable cooking skills.

Sports Day


Sports Day is an event focused on promoting physical activity, teamwork, and fun for the children at the orphanage.
Volunteers organize games like soccer, basketball, relay races, and tug-of-war. The event may also include sports-themed donations,
such as new equipment for the orphanage’s recreational activities.

Community Art Workshop


The Community Art Workshop provides a creative outlet for children at the orphanage and local community members to work together on an art project.
This event could include painting, sculpture, or crafting sessions.
The artwork created could be displayed at a local gallery or auctioned off to raise funds for the orphanage.

Holiday Celebration Party


The Holiday Celebration Party is an event where the orphanage hosts a festive gathering to celebrate Christmas, Eid, or other local holidays.
The children are treated to decorations, music, a gift exchange, and a holiday meal.
The event may also include performances by local entertainers and a special visit from Santa Claus or another holiday figure.

Adoption Awareness Campaign


The Adoption Awareness Campaign is an event to spread awareness about the children who are available for adoption.
The event includes presentations, sharing success stories of past adoptions, and providing information for prospective adoptive parents.
The goal is to find permanent homes for children in need.

Fitness for Kids Charity Run


Fitness for Kids is a charity run or walk designed to promote healthy living while raising money for the orphanage.
Participants can register for the run, with all the proceeds going to the orphanage.
The event can also have a fun, interactive element, such as a "Kids Run" for children to participate in alongside their parents.
