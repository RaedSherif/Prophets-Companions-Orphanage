About Our Board Games Program

Our board games activities offer children a chance to learn valuable skills while having fun. Through classic games like chess, checkers, and puzzle games, children develop cognitive abilities, patience, and social skills in a supportive and enjoyable environment.

Benefits of Board Games Activities

  • Enhances critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Teaches patience and turn-taking
  • Encourages healthy social interaction and teamwork
  • Builds resilience and adaptability
  • Provides a structured, screen-free activity

How to Get Involved

If you enjoy playing board games and want to make a positive impact, join us! Volunteers help guide children through games, teach strategies, and foster a positive, inclusive gaming environment.
