About the Caretaker Role

Caretakers play a crucial role in the daily lives of children, ensuring their physical, emotional, and mental well-being. They assist with routines, provide companionship, and contribute to a positive environment where children can thrive.

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Compassionate and patient attitude toward children
  • Experience in caregiving or working with children (preferred)
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Basic knowledge of child safety and first aid
  • Ability to engage in physical activities and assist in daily routines
