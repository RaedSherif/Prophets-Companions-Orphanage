How You Can Help

Join Us in Making a Difference in the Lives of Our Children
At Prophet’s Companions Orphanage, every bit of support counts in providing our children with the love, care, and opportunities they deserve. There are many ways you can contribute to our mission—whether through financial assistance, volunteering, or donating essential items. Explore the list below to see how you can help make a meaningful impact.

Financial Donations
Monetary donations allow us the flexibility to address urgent needs and fund essential programs for the children.
One-Time Donation: Your single donation can support immediate needs, such as medical care, food supplies, or educational resources.
Monthly Sponsorship: Join our family of regular donors by sponsoring a child or contributing to our monthly needs. Your consistent support will have a lasting impact on their lives.
To donate, please visit our donations page or contact us for more information.

Donate Essential Items
We’re always in need of specific items to maintain a safe, comfortable, and enriching environment for our children. Some essential items include:
School Supplies: Notebooks, pencils, textbooks, art supplies, and backpacks.
Clothing and Footwear: New or gently used clothes, especially in a variety of sizes, shoes, and seasonal wear.
Hygiene Products: Soap, shampoo, toothpaste, towels, and other hygiene essentials.
Medical Supplies: Over-the-counter medicines, first aid supplies, vitamins, and other health-related items.
Non-Perishable Food Items: Rice, pasta, canned goods, and other non-perishable foods.
Please contact us to confirm what items are currently most needed and to arrange drop-off or shipping.

Spread the Word
One of the simplest yet most effective ways to help is by sharing our mission with others. Whether through social media, community groups, or word of mouth, you can help us reach more people who may be inspired to support our children.
Follow Us on Social Media: Stay updated by following our official pages.
Host a Fundraiser: Consider hosting a fundraising event or campaign in your community.
Tell a Friend: Share our story with friends, family, and anyone who may want to get involved.
Together, we can create a network of supporters that empowers our children’s futures.
