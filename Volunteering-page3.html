About the Role

As an Art Instructor, you'll work with children of all ages, teaching them the fundamentals of drawing, painting, and other creative crafts. Your role is to inspire and nurture their artistic potential, providing a safe and supportive environment where they can express themselves freely.

Requirements

  • Experience in art, design, or education
  • Passion for working with children and fostering creativity
  • Ability to commit to a regular schedule
  • Patience, empathy, and strong communication skills
  • Background check and orientation (provided by us)
