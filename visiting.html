Visit Prophet’s Companions Orphanage

Experience the Difference Your Support Makes in the Lives of Our Children

We warmly welcome visitors to Prophet’s Companions Orphanage! Seeing our community, meeting our children, and experiencing the environment we’ve created can be a powerful way to understand our mission and see firsthand the positive impact your support brings.

What to Expect During Your Visit
Tour the Facilities: See our classrooms, play areas, dormitories, and community spaces where children learn, play, and grow.
Meet the Team: Get to know our dedicated staff and volunteers who are committed to the well-being of each child.
Engage with Our Programs: Experience our educational and life skills programs, learn about our approach to holistic development, and see how our core values are brought to life.
Spend Time with the Children: With supervised activities, you’ll have the chance to interact with the children, joining in games, crafts, or reading sessions.

Visiting Hours
Monday to Friday: 10:00 am - 4:00 pm
Saturday: 11:00 am - 3:00 pm
Sunday: Closed for visits
Note: To ensure a smooth visit and avoid overcrowding, please schedule your visit at least one week in advance.

Guidelines for Visitors
To maintain a safe, nurturing environment for our children, we kindly ask that all visitors adhere to the following guidelines:
Respect the Children’s Privacy: While we encourage interaction, please be mindful of the children’s personal boundaries.
Photography: For privacy and safety, photography is only allowed in certain areas. Please seek staff permission before taking any photos.
Dress Code: Modest attire is appreciated to show respect for our values and community.
Bringing Gifts or Donations: If you would like to bring gifts or make a donation, please check with our staff beforehand to see what items are currently needed.

Schedule Your Visit
To schedule your visit, please fill out our Visit Request Form below. Our team will contact you to confirm your visit and answer any additional questions you may have.
